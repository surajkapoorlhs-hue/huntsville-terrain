# Metro Bounds

## Included in v1

- Downtown Huntsville / Big Spring core
- Research Park / Bridge Street / Rocket Center corridor
- Redstone-facing western edge cues
- Monte Sano, Jones Valley, Hampton Cove, and Green Mountain transitions
- Madison and Town Madison / Toyota Field
- Northern city fabric up through Alabama A&M

## Why the bounds stop here

The first release is metro-scale, but not an all-direction suburban crawl. The boundary should hold the areas that materially shape how Huntsville is recognized: mountain ridges, major valleys, airport-facing and Redstone-facing corridors, the research / aerospace west side, and the Madison sports-commercial district.
